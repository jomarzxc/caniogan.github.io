TIPS FOR FIRE SAFETY

Use flashlights during power outages, not candles

Keep flammable items awau from anything that can get hot, such as space heaters.

Install smoke alarms on every level of your home, inside bedrooms,and outside sleeping areas.

Test smoke alarms monthly. Replace batteries at least once a year.

Have a family fire escapw plan and practice than twice a year.

Keep matches and lighters out of children’s reach.

Only use smoking materials outside.

If there’s a fire in your home,get out , dial 911 and don’t back inside.
