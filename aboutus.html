About Us!
Barangay Caniogan

About

Caniogan is a barangay located on the northern part of Pasig City. It is bounded on the south by Barangay Kapasigan, on the west by Marikina River, on the east by Barangays San Miguel and Sagad, and on the north by Barangay Maybunga

Welcome to our website

Our tapestry of cultures and traditions, and locals gather to celebrate our heritage while embracing modernity. Our community is well-known for its strong feeling of community and neighborly attitude, which fosters an environment in which everyone feels like family. We prioritize the well-being of our people at Brgy Caniogan, offering a variety of programs and activities to encourage education, health, and community development. Our devoted leaders and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that everyone has a safe, inclusive, and prospering workplace.
